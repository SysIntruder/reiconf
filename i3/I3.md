# I3 Ricing

## Disable PC Speaker
1. `sudo touch /etc/modprobe.d/nobeep.conf`
2. `sudo nvim /etc/modprobe.d/nobeep.conf`
3. add `blacklist pcpskr`
3. add `blacklist snd_pcsp`
